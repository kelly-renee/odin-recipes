Description

A delicious guacamole recipe using fresh ingredients! Add seasoning to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • avocados
  • cilantro
  • lemons
  • onion

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.

Description

Quick and easy pico de gallo that is a great addition to dinner!

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • red onion
  • jalapeno
  • lime
  • garlic
  • cumin

Steps

  1. Stir the tomatoes, onion, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, lime juice, garlic, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Refrigerate at least 3 hours before serving.

Description

This is a scrumptious taco dip. Serve with baked tortilla chips for dipping

Ingredients

  • cream cheese
  • sour cream
  • taco seasoning
  • cheddar cheese
  • green bell pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, sour cream and taco seasoning. Spread this mixture in a 9-inch (or a little larger) round serving dish. Top the mixture with lettuce, Cheddar cheese, tomatoes, bell pepper and black olives.
